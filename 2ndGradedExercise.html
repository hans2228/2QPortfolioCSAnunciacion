What is Clash Royale?

Clash Royale is a multiplayer strategy game in real-time and it is the creation of Supercell, the game creators of Clash of Clans. Players gather and upgrade cards of the Clash universe with troops, spells, and defenses into the game which they use to fight others in fast-paced duels. The aim is to bring down enemy towers and defend yours and get crowns and trophies and ascend the ranks. Players are able to join or form clans, exchange cards and take part in international tournaments. Clash Royale is strategic and exciting to players around the world due to continuous updates, seasonal events and new cards.
